RSA Project



video of rsa project



response to 3A

The overall purpose of the program is to encrypt and decrypt messages. The video demonstrates a word being encrypted then decrypted and at the end the use of delete buttons that delete text from some listboxes. The word dog was typed into an encryption box that encrypted the word. Next the encrypted version of dog was pasted into a decryption box and decrypted it back to its original form.


response to 3C

The two text boxes add an obvious and easy way to input the words that you want encrypted and decrypted. I developed two extra text boxes in my code. They are called text_box1 and text_box4. The first text box adds a way to input the word you want encrypted and the second one adds a way to input the encrypted version of the word to decrypt it.
