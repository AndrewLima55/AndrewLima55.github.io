Thunkable Project



video of rsa project



response to 3A

The overall purpose of the program is to encrypt and decrypt messages.

The inputs and outputs demonstrated in the video began with some E,N and D values being typed into some text boxes. Next the word dog being typed into an encryption text box and being encrypted after an encryption button was pressed. Next the encrypted text was passed into a decryption box and was decrypted after a decryption button was pressed. After that two delete buttons under the encryption and decryption list boxes were pressed removing text from both listboxes. Finally some encrypted text was saved to a file and reopened from that file using save and open buttons located in the menu.

The video begins by showing E, N and D values being typed into their appropriate text boxes and next a word being encrypted then decrypted. Next it shows the use of delete buttons that delete text from the listboxes. Finally it show the decrypted text getting saved to a file and then reopened from the file.
