Calculator GUI



Video of Calculator



response to 3A-3B

The video demonstrates a multiplication problem being solved and saved to a file. From there, the text box was cleared using a clear button and then reopened from the file. The video also demonstrated a list box and remove button for the list box. The list box was made to store answers to problems and the delete button removes unwanted answers.


The purpose of my program is to carry out arithmetic operations on numbers and be able to store your results.

The video showed an input of 5 times 5 and the output was 25. The video also showed the output of the textbox storing the solution 25 in the list box at the bottom. The text box was then cleared. Finally, when the open button was pressed, the stored number 25 was displayed in the text box.
